About

Cassandra Coleman, LCSW I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker known for creating a calm, compassionate, and grounded therapeutic space where clients feel safe to be seen, heard, and understood. My work focuses on supporting those navigating anxiety, depression, life transitions, parenting challenges, and the invisible load—along with serving veterans and individuals seeking clarity during pivotal moments in their lives. I believe therapy should feel both supportive and intentional. In our work together, I provide a validating environment while helping you gain insight, strengthen your voice, and move forward with purpose. This is a space where you don’t have to carry everything alone—and where clarity becomes possible.
